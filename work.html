Pollution

Causes of Pollution are on serious hike for Humanity. Following are some of the causes:-

  • Burning garbage
  • Emission
  • Transportation
  • Urbanization
  • Wildfire

Pollution is being a major cause for the extinction of many sea animals, whether they are turtle, sea fishes, shark etc.

Humanity needs to take direct action toward this critical issue and come with certain regulations to reduce the extinction of sea animals.
